# Snapshot Contract

Keep `UIElement` and `AccessibilitySnapshot` aligned with `accessibility-layer.md`.

`UIElement` fields:

- `role`
- `name`
- `value`
- `state`
- `bounds`
- `children`
- `source`

`AccessibilitySnapshot` fields:

- `timestamp`
- `platform`
- `backend_used`
- `focused_app`
- `focused_element`
- `tree`
- `raw_text`
- `confidence`
