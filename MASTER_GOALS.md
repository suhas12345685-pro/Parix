# MASTER_GOALS

Parix reads this file during every proactive Pulse. Goals here should be concrete
enough to schedule useful background work, but broad enough to survive daily
changes.

## Business

- Build a Hyderabad local-business lead engine, starting with preschools and
  service businesses.
- Prepare outreach drafts only after researching the business, its website, and
  a real problem Parix can help solve.
- Keep unfinished lead lists and pitch drafts in `shadow_drafts/business/` until
  they are ready for review.

## Academic

- Protect Grade 10 mastery in Physics, Math, and English.
- Watch for consented deadlines and exams, then suggest study plans early enough
  to be useful.
- Keep unfinished study notes and research drafts in `shadow_drafts/academic/`.

## Technical

- Maintain and improve the JARVIS/Parix codebase.
- Prioritize reliability, safety, cognition, tests, and demo readiness.
- Keep speculative refactor notes, generated scripts, and investigation drafts
  in `shadow_drafts/technical/` until they are ready to promote.

## Pulse Rules

- No proactive cron task may run more often than every 15 minutes.
- Every autonomous task must name a goal, data source, and deliverable.
- Destructive, public, credential, financial, or external-message actions require
  explicit confirmation.
